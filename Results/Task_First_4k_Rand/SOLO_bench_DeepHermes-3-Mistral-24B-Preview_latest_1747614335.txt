1. What are the four fundamental physical forces? Gravity, Electromagnetism, Strong Nuclear Force, and Weak Nuclear Force.
2. Who discovered neutrons? James Chadwick in 1932.
3. What is the chemical reaction that transforms glucose into energy? Cellular respiration.
4. Which planet has an atmosphere richest in carbon dioxide (CO₂)? Venus with about 96% CO₂ in its atmosphere.
5. What gas makes up the majority of Earth's atmosphere? Nitrogen constitutes approximately 78% of Earth's atmosphere.
