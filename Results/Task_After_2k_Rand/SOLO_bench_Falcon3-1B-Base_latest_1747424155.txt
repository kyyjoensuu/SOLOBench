1. Each day at 9 AM local central time, input one new sentence
2. Study different genres and styles to get ideas for your own work. Read widely and explore various types of writing to help inspire new directions.
3. Focus on specific goals or categories. This can help keep the writing focused, organized and clear from confusion about what topic is being discussed.
4. Keep track of commonly used vocabulary. Writing assignments that involve identifying and analyzing common words with similar meanings may help improve your ability to recognize these similarities and avoid repetition.
5. Take breaks between tasks or when working on important work. This can help refresh your mind and allow you to approach problems with fresh perspective.
6. Read feedback from others or use writing exercises. Reading feedback about a topic and actively practicing the skills involved can help you improve the quality of your writing over time.
